Analysis

The Alaska state website looks relatively old, with a traditional layout. The body of the website feels crammed, with lots of detailed images occupying a small space, but the whole page tends to keep a constant theme of the State's colors. I,m not a fan of the sites navigation. There are too many buttons to click on, and many buttons seem to have the same function. All navigation should be centralized. The order of the website is good. There is a lot of general information about the state on the home page that should be easy to access.

  • Order: 7 / 10

Analysis

The Iowa state website has a nice theme which appeals to the eye, and a cool animation upon arrival. However it is not formatted correctly, and it seems that 80% zoom is really what 100% should look like. As for the navigation, it is pretty simple. There is a nav bar at the top with all of the links necessary. There are also quick links which give easy access to people with common specific purposes. The organization of the site is intuitive with the links connecting to other pages. Further down and not immediately visible are other features which are of less importance.
