My Recommendation

Based on the minimal research I have done, I would recommend Hostinger for anyone who has minimal web hosting needs. It offers the cheapest plans. However, for a very large business, it seems that Hostwinds would be the better option because it offers dedicated servers and seems to focus more on reliability than the others.
